First class accomodation in the Southern Cotswolds

Situated in the village of Tormarton, with rural views from all windows and the Cotswold Way bounding the property, Noades Studio is the ideal spot to relax and start exploring the Cotswolds.

For Business

Noades Studio is ideally located for working in Bath, Bristol, Chippenham or Swindon with the M4 just a few minutes drive from the property and great wifi if you need to work remotely.

For Pleasure

The Cotswold Way runs past the foot of the garden, the World Heritage city of Bath is just over 10 miles away, and the area is surrounded by beautiful countryside and National Trust properties.

The Studio

The Studio comprises a light and airy, double height, fully fitted kitchen/living room with French windows onto the garden together with an exposed beam double bedroom and en-suite toilet and shower room.

What’s included?

A Welcome Hamper of local produce including milk, all heating and lighting, bed linen and towels. We can also provide home cooked meals, a laundry service or even a fully stocked kitchen as optional extras.

The living space

The kitchen includes a full size oven, a microwave, a fridge-freezer, dishwasher and ceramic hob (as well as all the crockery etc.). The adjoining sitting area includes the sofa, TV and DVD player and French windows providing direct access to the garden.

The bedroom

A double bed with bedside tables, lighting and radio. There’s plenty of storage space in the cupboard, and a peaceful view over the garden and fields towards the village.

The bathroom

The en-suite bathroom includes the shower cubicle, toilet & basin, shaver socket and large wall mounted mirror. All towels provided.

The garden

As well as the garden surrounding the property, you’ll have access to your own patio area with seating and table. There’s also plenty of parking space, and a barbecue you can use when the sun’s shining.
