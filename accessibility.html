Introduction

Single storey Self catering accommodation with private driveway.

Pre-Arrival

Comprehensive standards-compliant website with email and telephone contact details.

Arrival & Car Parking Facilities

Gravel driveway with 2 natural stone steps to front door under covered porch. Private parking on driveway with stopping off available immediately in front of entrance.

Main Entrance, Reception & Ticketing Area

All accommodation is on one level within the property.

Public Areas - Hall, Stairs, Landing, Corridors etc

All areas are private to the property. Quarry tiled floors in kitchen and bathroom. Fitted carpets in living area and bedroom.

Public Areas - Sitting room, lounges, lobbies etc

See section above.

Outdoor Facilities

There is a concreted patio area accessed via a small step from the gravel driveway.

Bedrooms & Sleeping Areas

The bed has access on either side and at one end. There is a central light and also 2 touch-sensitive bedside lights.

Bathroom, Shower-room & WC

The bathroom is ensuite to the bedroom. It has a quarry tiled floor, and safety mat available for the shower tray. Wall hung basin and toilet.

Self-Catering Kitchen

The property opens from the front door into the fully integrated kitchen area which has a quarry tiled floor. There is a breakfast bar with two adjustable swivel stools. One and a half bowl sink with mixer tap.

Grounds and Gardens

The Studio has views to open countryside from all windows and is set in a large well maintained garden which can be accessed by visitors by request with the owner. The Studio has its own small courtyard space with a table and 2 chairs for exclusive use.

Additional Information

A fire risk assessment has been carried out and is available to view if requested.
No animals are allowed and this is a no smoking property.

Hours of operation

365 days a year
