Finding us

When you arrive, keys can be collected from Noades House.

The nearest train station is Yate, but Bath Spa, Chippenham, Bristol Temple Meads and Bristol Parkway are all within a 30 minute drive. If you’d like to arrange a transfer to or from any local train stations, just let us know and we’ll be glad to help.
