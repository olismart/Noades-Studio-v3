Booking Information

Prices

Prices start at just £320 for a full week. Most bookings start on a Friday, but if you have any other requirements just get in touch.

How to book

Email us at hi@noadesstudio.co.uk or phone 01454 218 278 (or just use the form at the bottom of the page) and let us know the dates you’d like to stay. We’ll be in touch quickly to let you know how much your stay will cost.

The reservation will be held for one week and bookings should be confirmed by sending a transfer or cheque for one third of the price within this week.

The full balance to be paid six weeks before your arrival. We can’t currently accept credit card payments, but we can give you the details for Paypal or bank transfer.

The reservation will be held for one week and bookings should be confirmed by sending a cheque for one third of the price within this week. For electronic transfer of funds please contact us. Balance to be paid six weeks before your arrival.

Get in touch

If there’s anything else you’d like to know about the studio or the area, you can drop us an email, phone us on (+44) 01454 218 278 or just use the form and we’ll get back to you.
